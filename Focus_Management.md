# Focus Management 

### Question 1: What is Deep Work?
* Deep work is focusing without any distraction on a task that is very hard for the brain.
* It is like an "athletic skill" for your mind that helps you produce high-value results.

### Question 2: According to the author, how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
* **Set Boundaries:** Schedule specific times for distractions so they don't leak into your focus time.
* **Build a Ritual:** Create a regular habit or "rhythm" for your deep work sessions to avoid wasting energy deciding when to start.
* **Limit the Time:** Realize that even experts can only do about four hours of deep work a day.
* **Shutdown Properly:** Have a clear routine to end your workday so your brain can fully rest.

### Question 4: What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
* **Kills Concentration:** It trains your brain to be constantly distracted, making it hard to focus on deep tasks later.
* **Mental Health Issues:** Too much use is linked to feeling lonely, inadequate, and depressed.
* **Increased Anxiety:** It creates a "background hum" of constant anxiety because our brains aren't meant for constant digital rewards.
* **Economic Risk:** If you lose your ability to focus, you become less valuable in a competitive job market.
