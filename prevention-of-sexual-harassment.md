1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Unwanted touching like hugging or holding someone without permission.
- Sexual jokes or comments about someone’s body or looks.
- Sending sexual messages, pictures, or emails.
- Asking for sexual favours.
- Staring, gestures, or actions that make someone feel uncomfortable.


2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

- Stay calm and make sure the person affected feels supported.
- Tell the person to stop if it is safe to do so.
- Do not ignore the behavior, especially if it happens repeatedly.
- Report the incident to a trusted person, teacher, manager, or the proper authority.
- Support the person affected and encourage them to speak up.
